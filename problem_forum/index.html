The Alliance of Computer Science Students

The Alliance of Computer Science Students - University of the Philippines Los Banos (ACSS UPLB) is a duly recognized service-oriented academic student organization which aims to assist in building a group of individuals who shall lead in solving the different problems of the community using technology, especially the computing sciences, as its main tool. In this regard, we are conducting our first major activity in our first year of existence.

The 8Layer Academy

8layer Academy is a modern educational institution that aims to empower citizens by offering people opportunities to develop their personal passions, advance their careers, lead in their professions and learn things through the collaboration of the community, academe, government, civil society and corporations.

About the Problem Forum

The ACSS Problem Forum is intended to scrutinize and give a perspective to the different fields and emerging technologies of computer science, in the aim of solving the different problems of society. In its inception, the Alliance of Computer Science Students aims to use technology as its main tool in solving the issues of today. As it aims to be an annual activity of the Alliance, we enjoin students and professionals from all other disciplines to give solutions to the country's local problems. In this way, we can craft ideas and innovations that shall be our global solutions.
